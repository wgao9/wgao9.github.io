Biography

Dr. Weihao Gao is a research scientist in the ByteDance applied machinge learning (AML) research team at Seattle. His research interest lies in maching learning, deep learning, recommendation system and information theory. He received Ph.D. from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Univeristy of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2019, working under the supervision of Prof. Sewoong Oh and Prof. Pramod Viswanath. Prior to that, he received M.S. from the same department at UIUC in 2016, and B.E. in Computer Science from Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (known as Yao Class) at Tsinghua University in 2014.
