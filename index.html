Biography

Weihao Gao will join the applied machinge learning (AML) research team of ByteDance Inc. in Septemer 2019. His research interest lies in maching learning, deep learning, information theory and their applications. He received Ph.D. from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Univeristy of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2019, working under the supervision of Prof. Sewoong Oh and Prof. Pramod Viswanath. Prior to that, he received M.S. from the same department at UIUC in 2016, and B.E. in Computer Science from Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University in 2014.
